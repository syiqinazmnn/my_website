Connection Between The Words: Pen Pal Program

Organized by the UiTM Language Club, this pen pal program ran for about a month. It was an anonymous exchange where I used the pseudonym "Peanut" and my partner went by "Lucy." We exchanged letters through a mediator, who ensured our anonymity until the reveal on 1st December 2023.

Through this program, I experienced the joy of getting to know someone solely through writing letters. We exchanged thoughts on music, TV shows, and life in general, despite having opposite tastes. When we finally met during a Google Meet session, it was both awkward and exciting! We bonded over our shared love for BTS and even exchanged social media handles.
